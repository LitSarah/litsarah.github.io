I'm Sarah Little and I am a budding analyst. I make knowledge accessable to everyone.

A healthy interest in technology runs in my blood. My grandfather repaired televisions for a living. My uncle's a tinkerer. My brother administers virtual machines. And days out with my mother usually involved the gentle hum and chill air of server rooms.

From the days of C:/Windows run to beautiful data-driven web pages, I seek to expand my skills in fun and interactive ways. My side projects generally involve one of my many other hobbies, such as photography, swing dancing, and bartending, which are generally more interesting than the retail systems I deal with during the day.
